## Abstract

We present **FollowLang**, a suite of seven specialized meta-programming languages designed for Quantum Gravity Turing-Computing (QGTC) on the **CanorOS** operating system. Rather than compiling statements into traditional register machine instructions, FollowLang compiles declarations into diffeomorphisms, cohomologies, and topological transformations evaluated by the **Follow** math engine. We detail the syntax, semantics, and compilation pipelines of these languages, showing how they translate high-level design specifications ("vibe-coding" under **AetherAI**) into secure, error-mitigated execution states verified by **Envoy**.

## 2. Language Definitions and Compilation Pipeline

Throughout all of these, machine-epsilon update-/error-rate ratios are monitored and mitigated, using the `Project Novus`, `Project Tempus`, `Project Canor`, `Project Aether` and `Project Envoy` Follow-Projects (with `CanorOS` as the foundation for these projects).

### 2.1 Flow™: Topological Permutation Strings
- **Syntax**: Strings composed of imaginary unit steps `{ ±1_n, ±i_n, ±j_n, ±k_n }` or `{ R_n, B_n }` where $n$ represents the Hamiltonian ring index.
- **Compilation**: Translates directly to a doubly-linked list of ringed bases (`BASE`), defining the coordinate geometry of local Lorentzian frames.

### 2.2 Action™: Moduli-Space Boundaries
- **Syntax**: Modular arithmetic constraints, e.g., `(7m + (4n * 4o)) == 0 (mod 3)`.
- **Compilation**: Generates filtering rules that are applied to `Flow` states, restricting calculations to physically valid, non-divergent spaces.

### 2.3 Maneuvers™: Non-Commutative Meta-Groups
- **Syntax**: Weighted functional mappings over Flow and Action spaces.
- **Compilation**: Establishes machine learning boundary heuristics. Used by AetherAI to optimize code generation paths.

### 2.4 United Interchange™: Orthonormal Protocols
- **Semantics**: An interchange language that uses reservoir computing to discover orthonormal, dataset-independent workflows across distributed systems.

### 2.5 Elite™: Bare-Metal QGTC Assembly
- **Syntax**: Symbolic instructions restricted to imperative procedurals, like: `mov`, `jmp`, and `cmp` operating on spacetime origo deltas.
- **Compilation**: Assembled into raw TST node operations.

### 2.6 Follower™: High-Level Complementary Language
- **Semantics**: The front-end framework that integrates custom mathematical rules with other standard programming constructs.

### 2.7 Ether™: The Intermediate Representation (IR)
- **Semantics**: The final linking language. It performs machine-epsilon error mitigation, rounding fuzzy boundary values to maintain algebraic stability across systems.
